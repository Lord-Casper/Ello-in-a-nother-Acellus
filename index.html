Overview

Acellus is an online learning platform used primarily for K-12 education. It offers a range of courses across various subjects including mathematics, science, language arts, social studies, and more. The platform is designed to be flexible and adaptive, catering to the individual needs of students.

Key Features

  • Curriculum: Wide variety of courses aligned with state and national standards.
  • Adaptive Learning: Personalized instruction based on student performance.
  • Video Lessons: Lessons created by experienced teachers, often with interactive elements.
  • Assessments: Quizzes and tests to monitor student progress.
  • Special Programs: Includes STEM education and credit recovery.
  • Teacher Support: Comprehensive dashboard for monitoring progress and providing feedback.
  • Accessibility: Available on various devices including computers, tablets, and smartphones.
  • Parental Involvement: Parent interface for tracking student progress.
